# ChronoSquares Brand Palette — Obsidian Forest

## Dark theme (public site)
- Primary background: `#0D1312` (Deep Obsidian Green)
- Card background: `#1A2421` (Subtle Dark Forest)
- Borders & dividers: `#26322E` (Deep Teal-Gray)
- Primary accent / buttons: `#8AB79B` (Sage/Mint Green) — `--brand`
- Soft glow / highlights: `#2D4A3E` (Emerald Bloom) — `--brand-violet` (secondary accent slot)
- Heading text: `#FFFFFF` (pure white)
- Body text: `#E2E8F0` (light grey-white)
- Muted text: `#9CA3AF` (Mist Gray)

## Light theme (app)
- Sage primary stays the brand: deeper saturated sage `oklch(0.58 0.08 158)` for AA contrast on white
- Emerald Bloom remains accent
- Warm-paper background, white cards
